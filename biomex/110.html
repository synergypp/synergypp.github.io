DCP FC 110

The effective and friendly way of cleaning

Characteristics

  • DCP® FC 110 is an additive with an antifoam effect for all foaming DCP® products;
  • Extremely efficient;
  • Contains silicones (Not useful in applications for painting industry);
  • Stable in all Biomex Products.

User's instruction

  • Add Biomex ANTIFOAM (undiluted) to the cleaning liquid in proportion of   10 ml per 100 liters cleaning liquid, depending on the procedure;
  • When foam occurs again, re-dose slightly.
